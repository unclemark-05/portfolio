# The Scripture Call Journey: Faith Meets Technology

Scripture Call started as a simple idea: what if people could receive a personalized Bible verse every day, delivered right to their phone?

## Why Scripture Call?

In a world full of notifications and distractions, many people struggle to maintain a consistent Bible reading habit. Scripture Call removes the friction by bringing the Word directly to you.

## Building the Platform

### Frontend Architecture

The website needed to be fast, accessible, and SEO-optimized. I chose:

- **Next.js** for static generation and SEO
- **Tailwind CSS** for responsive design
- **Schema.org markup** for rich search results

### Content Strategy

With **12 Bible topic categories** and the full KJV Bible, content organization was crucial. I implemented:

- Topic-based navigation
- Reading plans with progress tracking
- Search functionality across all scripture

### SEO Deep Dive

For a content-heavy site like Scripture Call, SEO was critical:

```
- Structured data (JSON-LD) for articles
- Dynamic sitemap generation
- Optimized meta tags per page
- Fast Core Web Vitals scores
```

## Key Features

1. **Daily Calls** — Users receive automated calls at their preferred time
2. **12 Topic Categories** — From Healing to Prosperity to Salvation
3. **Reading Plans** — Guided scripture reading journeys
4. **Full KJV Bible** — Complete searchable Bible text

## Impact

Scripture Call has helped hundreds of users build consistent Bible reading habits. The combination of technology and faith creates a unique value proposition.
